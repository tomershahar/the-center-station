 # The Center Station                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                         
  A sci-fi comedy point-and-click adventure game built with Godot 4 and the Popochiu plugin.                             
                                                                                                                         
  ## About                                                                                                             
                                                                                                                         
  You are Nathe (or Nathali), a space PhD stranded aboard a research station after something                           
  has gone very wrong. Your only companions: Roger, your blunt robot assistant, and ARIA,                                
  the station AI whose rigid protocol adherence makes her more obstacle than ally.                                       
                                                                                                                         
  Explore 11 interconnected rooms, solve bureaucratic puzzles, negotiate with an intelligent                             
  alien organism, and figure out what exactly counts as a "valid research justification."                                
                                                                                                                         
  **Genre:** Sierra-style point-and-click adventure                                                                      
  **Playtime:** ~60 minutes                                                                                              
  **Platform:** PC (Windows / macOS / Linux)                                                                             
  **Engine:** Godot 4 + Popochiu                                                                                         
                                                                                                                         
  ## Features                                                                                                          
                                                                                                                         
  - Classic point-and-click mechanics with a modern engine                                                             
  - Configurable hint system via Roger (talkative / balanced / quiet)                                                    
  - Branching dialogue and inventory-based puzzles                                                                     
  - Six science labs, each with its own puzzle chain                                                                     
  - Dry comedy throughout                           
                                                                                                                         
  ## Status                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                         
  Early development — placeholder art, core rooms implemented.
